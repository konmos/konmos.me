# Hello

When I came to the UK I had absolutely zero knowledge or experience with English but somehow managed to learn it within about a year to an acceptable standard. I started programming when I was around 13 years old - initially in Java but dropped it for Python not too long after. Python is my language of choice as I have the most experience with it. Currently, I'm a student at Queen's University, Belfast, studying MEng Computer Science and will be graduating in 2023.
